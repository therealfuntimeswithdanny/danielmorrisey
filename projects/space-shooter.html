Space Shooter is a simple Space Invaders clone written in Python using Pygame. The game can run on MacOS in five simple steps, Windows support coming soon.


Space Shooter works with


Install

Step 1

Download for MacOS

.tar (recomended) .zip


Step 2

Look for your downloads folder on your dock

Open the downloaded file, it should be called space-shooter-public.tar or space-shooter-public.zip


Step 3

When it's opening the .tar or .zip you will be brought to a scrren like this, STOP once you get here!


Step 4

I don't feel like paying $100/year so apple can let people use my apps so in the mean time we need to do this.


Open system settings witch can be done by pressing CMD + SPACE on your keyboard, then type in settings and hit enter.


Once you are in settings, click on Privacy & Security and scroll down to the bottom of the page.


Click on the button that says Open Anyway and then click on Open in the pop-up.


Now you can play Space Shooter!


Step 5

To play Space Shooter, open the space-shooter-public folder from your downloads folder, then open the dist folder & drag the Second Space Shooter (it has a ship icon) over to your applications folder on the left.


Now if you press CMD + SPACE and type in "Space Shooter" click the Space Ship icon to luanch Space Shooter!
