Space Shooter (v1.2.3)

This game is a simple Space Invaders clone.

Install

Step 1: Download for MacOS

Choose your preferred download format:

.app (Best Option) .tar .zip
Step 2: Locate and Open

Look for the app named `Space Shooter` and double-click to open it.

Step 3: Handle Unidentified Developer Warning

A pop-up will appear stating the app is from an unidentified developer. This is because I am not a registered Apple Developer. Simply click Done.

Step 4: Bypass Security Settings

Since I don't pay for an Apple Developer license, you need to manually allow the app. Here's how:

  • Open **System Settings** by pressing CMD + SPACE on your keyboard, typing `settings`, and hitting enter.
  • Navigate to **Privacy & Security** and scroll to the bottom.
Step 5: Open Anyway and Play

Once you click Open Anyway, the game window will appear and you can start playing! Remember that you'll need to repeat **Step 2** each time you want to play the game.
